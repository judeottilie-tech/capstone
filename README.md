<h1>portfol.io</h1>
The goal and purpose of portfol. io, and the problems it solves:
I have used many art commission websites where they throw you in with all the other artists on the site. You have to have the most eye-catching listing among the search results or else you won't receive any commissions. On the other hand, there are sites where you can make your own art portfolio, but having commission listings is out of the question.
I wanted to make a portfolio-like site where an artist could receive commission proposals from a guest viewing their page. The goal was for it to be a site ideal for business cards, with a simple URL like portfol. io/artist. The guest viewing the artists' profile would not need an account to send a commission inquiry. This makes it so that the artists' individual page looks more like a personal portfolio, but gives the artist the power to accept commissions in the same place. 

Let me know what you think! I hope to grow this project as I learn more.
